About

Three decades of journalism,
now working for you

Fred Pals is an experienced journalist and content strategist with more than 30 years in international media. After 16 years at Bloomberg News and stints at the Wall Street Journal and Dow Jones Newswires, he founded Pals Media to bring that same editorial rigor to branded content.

Pals Media specializes in branded journalism — partner content that reads like quality journalism because it is written by a journalist. From thought leadership and impact reports to opeds and LinkedIn strategy, every piece is crafted with the same standards of accuracy, clarity, and storytelling that define the best newsrooms.

  • 16 years at Bloomberg News
  • Wall Street Journal / Dow Jones Newswires
  • 30+ years of international journalism
  • Based in the Netherlands, working globally
